📅 Day 1 — Sat

Centre · sights · Corniche swim
Morning
Coffee/breakfast at Petrin Couchette or Pollux (3× rec). Wander the Vieux-Port → Noailles market.
Lunch
⚠ book Coquille (4× — "back twice for the mussels"), or cheap & authentic Chez Yassine in Noailles.
Afternoon
Notre-Dame de la Garde (4×, best views) → bus 83 down the Corniche → swim at Plage de Maldormé / Malmousque → coffee at Boujou or Tarlata Café.
Evening
Sunset drink Café de l'Abbaye (3×) → walk up to dinner at La Relève ⚠ book. Big-night alt: Limmat ⚠ book (7×).
Late
Bars around Cours Julien (4×) / Bar Gaspard (4.8★).

📅 Day 2 — Sun

South coast · calanques
Late morning → lunch
South (bus 19 or the La Navette ferry) to Les Goudes / Tuba Club ⚠ book (4×, seafront) — lunch + swim at Baie des Singes or a calanque. Alt: day-trip to Cassis.
Afternoon
Swim/relax at the Calanques or wander back via Vallon des Auffes.
Evening
Wine + small plates at Figure (4×) or Mercato. Dinner Le Vin sur la Main ⚠ book via IG (4×) — check it's reopened.
Book today: Limmat, Coquille, Tuba, Le Vin sur la Main.
Sunday caution: many Marseille restaurants close Sun/Mon — Day 2 leans on beaches, calanques & sights (always open).
